What's inside

Four tools. One unfair advantage.

Everything a sharp bettor needs to squeeze value out of every offer, every match and every odds movement.

01 — ARBITRAGE RADAR

Spot guaranteed-profit windows in real time

The radar continuously compares odds across bookmakers and flags matches where the combined margin drops near — or below — zero. Below 0% means a locked-in profit.

  • ✓ Live margin on every match, sorted tightest first
  • ✓ Best odds per outcome, per bookmaker, side by side
  • ✓ Filter by sport: football, basketball, and more
02 — FREEBETS OPTIMIZER

Get the most out of every free bet

Pick your bookmakers, enter your free-bet amount, and Dycers scans the board to surface the single best-value bet — ranked by expected return and confidence.

  • ✓ AI-estimated win probability on every pick
  • ✓ Expected value in € so you know what it's worth
  • ✓ "Top deal" and high-confidence flags at a glance
03 — LIVE BET TRACKING

Track every open bet, catch every edge

Add your bets and Dycers follows them live — monitoring scores and odds so you never miss an arbitrage or hedging opportunity while a match is running.

  • ✓ Every active bet with stake, odds & bookmaker
  • ✓ Automatic arbitrage detection as odds move
  • ✓ Add a bet in seconds — search any team or league
04 — STATS & HISTORY

See your real numbers, not your feelings

A full history of every settled bet with win rate and net gain — the honest performance dashboard that tells you whether your strategy is actually working.

  • ✓ Net profit & win rate across all your bets
  • ✓ Every bet logged with odds, bookmaker & result
  • ✓ Final scores and per-bet profit at a glance
